About Us

We've brought technology and transport together to build mobility for a billion people. The SkyCabs mobile app has redefined point-to-point transportation in more than 100 cities across India. It is the quickest way to book a cab to anywhere in the city, right on your smartphone.

With multiple categories of cabs - Hatchback,Sedan, Prime - available at the tap of a button, SkyCabs has changed the way people travel. Categories like Share, Pool and Shuttle have made daily commute effortless and eco-friendly. We’ve partnered with local and traditional transport services like Auto rickshaws, Mumbai’s Kaali-Peeli and Kolkata’s yellow taxis, making them an integral part of our journey. We’ve also enabled TaxiForSure services on the SkyCabs app to bring make more cabs available for our users.

We’ve empowered several driver-entrepreneurs with great earning opportunities and support systems like Sky Pragati. We're also helping small to big organisations ease employee transport with Sky Corporate.

The Sky team is constantly innovating to bring you new and affordable ways to travel. We're focused on improving the transportation landscape while contributing to social and economic growth.
